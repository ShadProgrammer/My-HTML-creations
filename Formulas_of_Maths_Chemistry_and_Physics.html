Mathematcal equations:-

There are a lot of Mathematical equations in which few are described as follows,

1. This formula is known as Section formula used to find out co-ordinates of mid-points.This is used when there is Internal division of a line.
(m1x2 + m2x1) m1 + m2

2. This formula is same as above but used when there is External division of a line.
(m1x2 - m2x1) m1 - m2

3. This equation is used when two lines are perpendicular to each other and we need to find slopes.(m1 and m2 are slopes here)
m1.m2= -1
